The Best Free Math Tools Online

Published on September 12, 2025 • Category: Math Resources

Math can be challenging, but with the right tools, it becomes much easier and more fun. Here’s a list of the best free math tools online that students can use to practice, learn, and check their work.

1. Desmos (Graphing Calculator)

Desmos is a free, powerful online graphing calculator. Plot equations, visualize functions, and explore interactive graphs to understand math concepts better.

Tip: Use Desmos to check your homework graphs and experiment with equations.

2. GeoGebra

GeoGebra is perfect for geometry, algebra, and calculus. Its interactive tools allow you to manipulate shapes, use sliders, and visualize math dynamically.

3. Wolfram Alpha

Wolfram Alpha isn’t just a calculator—it’s a math knowledge engine. It can solve equations, show steps, and answer many types of math questions.

4. Khan Academy

Khan Academy provides free lessons and practice for everything from basic arithmetic to advanced calculus. It’s like having a personal tutor at your fingertips.

5. Symbolab

Symbolab is great for step-by-step solutions in algebra, calculus, and statistics. Use it to understand how to solve problems, not just get answers.

6. Mathway

Mathway solves almost any type of math problem. It’s perfect for checking work, but remember to learn the steps for better understanding.

Remember: Use these tools to learn and understand concepts, not just to get answers!
