How to Play Pattern Planet!

Welcome to Pattern Planet! Your mission is to remember and repeat the sparkling light patterns on the magical planet.

  • ✨ **Watch Carefully:** The planet will show you a sequence of flashing lights. Remember the order!
  • ✨ **Your Turn:** After the planet finishes, it's your turn to click the spots in the *exact same order*.
  • ✨ **Rotate the Planet:** Click and drag (or touch and drag) on the planet to spin it around and see all the spots!
  • ✨ **Zoom In/Out:** Use your mouse scroll wheel to zoom the camera!
  • ✨ **Level Up!** Get the pattern right, and you'll go to the next level with a new, longer pattern!
  • ✨ **Have Fun!** Let's see how many patterns you can remember!

Game Modes:

  • ✨ **Tutorial:** The planet plays a simple pattern, then it's your turn to repeat it.
  • ✨ **Easy:** Start with short patterns, great for beginners!
  • ✨ **Intermediate:** Longer patterns for a good challenge!
  • ✨ **Hard:** Test your memory with very long and fast patterns!
  • ✨ **Song:** A boss level! Play 10 musical levels, repeating segments of a song!
