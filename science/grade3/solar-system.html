What is the Solar System? ü§î

The **Solar System** is our neighborhood in space! It is made up of the **Sun** and everything that travels around it, including our planet Earth. The Sun is a giant, hot star at the center of our Solar System.

There are eight planets that orbit the Sun. They are: Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune. There are also smaller objects like dwarf planets (like Pluto!), moons, and asteroids.
