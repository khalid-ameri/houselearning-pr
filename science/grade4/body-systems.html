The **human body** is like a big team. It is made of many different parts that all work together. We call these parts **body systems**.

Each system has a special job to do. For example, the **Skeletal System** is the bones that hold you up. The **Digestive System** helps you eat and get energy. These systems all cooperate to keep you healthy!
