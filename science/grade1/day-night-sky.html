Why do we have Day and Night? ü§î

The Earth is always spinning, just like a top! This spinning is called **rotation**. As the Earth rotates, one side faces the Sun, and that side has **daytime**.

At the same time, the other side of the Earth faces away from the Sun, and that side has **nighttime**. It takes 24 hours for the Earth to make one full spin, which is why we have a day and a night every single day.
