What is Matter? ü§î

**Matter** is anything that has **mass** and takes up **space**. Everything around you is made of matter! The chair you're sitting on, the air you're breathing, and even the phone or computer you're using are all examples of matter.

A **property of matter** is a characteristic that we can use to describe something. We can observe these properties with our five senses. For example, a property of a basketball is that it's round, bouncy, and orange. These are all things we can see and feel!
