Building Blocks of Life! ü§î

A **cell** is the basic building block of all living things. Everything you see that's alive, from a tiny bug to a giant whale, is made of one or more cells. Some living things, like bacteria, are just one cell! Others, like you, have trillions of cells working together.

Cells are super, super small. You can't see them with just your eyes. You need a **microscope** to look at them. Every cell has special parts that help it do its job.
