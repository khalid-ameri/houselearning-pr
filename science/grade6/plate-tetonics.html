Earth's Moving Puzzle Pieces ü§î

The Earth's outer layer, called the **lithosphere**, is not one solid piece. It's broken into huge puzzle pieces called **tectonic plates**. These plates are always moving, floating on top of a hot, flowing layer of rock underneath called the **asthenosphere**. This movement is what we call **plate tectonics**.
