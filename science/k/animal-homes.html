Animal Homes Lesson (Text Version)

A **bird** builds a home called a **nest** high up in a tree. A busy **bee** lives in a big home with lots of friends called a **hive**. A big **bear** sleeps in a cozy place called a **den** for the winter. A **spider** weaves a beautiful, sticky home called a **web**. A **rabbit** digs a hole in the ground for a home called a **burrow**.
