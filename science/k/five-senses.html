Welcome to the Five Senses! 👋

Let's learn all about how our amazing bodies see, hear, smell, taste, and touch the world around us!

Video Lesson (Text Version)

We're learning with a fun story! Imagine you're a superhero who uses all your senses to explore.

Your eyes help you SEE big red apples 🍎 and beautiful flowers 🌷. Your ears help you HEAR birds singing 🐦 and your favorite song 🎶. Your nose helps you SMELL yummy cookies baking 🍪 and fresh cut grass 🌱. Your tongue helps you TASTE sweet ice cream 🍦 and sour lemons 🍋. And your hands help you TOUCH a soft teddy bear 🧸 and a bumpy rock 🪨.
