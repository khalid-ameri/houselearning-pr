Parts of a Plant Lesson (Text Version)

The **roots** go into the ground and drink water for the plant. The **stem** helps the plant stand up tall and brings water from the roots to the leaves. The **leaves** are like the plant's kitchen. They use sunlight to make food! The pretty **flower** helps to make seeds so new plants can grow. The **seed** is the little part that can grow into a new plant!
