**Weather** is what the sky is doing right now! It can be sunny, rainy, windy, or cloudy. Weather can change from day to day, or even from hour to hour!

**Climate** is what the weather is usually like in a place over a very, very long time. For example, the climate in Florida is usually warm and sunny, but the climate in Alaska is usually cold and snowy. Climate is like a place's personality, and weather is like its mood for the day.
