What is a Plant Life Cycle? ü§î

Just like people and animals, plants have a **life cycle**. This means they are born, they grow up, and they make new plants. It's a special journey that happens over and over again!

Most plants start as a **seed**, then grow roots and a stem to become a **seedling**. Next, they grow into a **mature plant** with flowers. The flowers make **new seeds** to start the cycle all over again!
