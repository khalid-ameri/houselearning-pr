What is the Water Cycle? ü§î

The **water cycle** is how water moves all around our planet. It goes up into the sky and then comes back down to the earth. It's a special journey that never stops!

The four main steps are: **Evaporation**, where water turns into a gas and goes up; **Condensation**, where it forms clouds; **Precipitation**, where it falls as rain or snow; and **Collection**, where it gathers in rivers, lakes, and oceans.
