What are Magnets and Force? ü§î

A **magnet** is a special kind of object that can pull on or push away certain metals without even touching them! This push or pull is called **force**.

Every magnet has two ends, called **poles**: a North pole (N) and a South pole (S). When you put two magnets together, **opposite poles** (like N and S) will **attract** or pull each other. But when you put two of the **same poles** together (like N and N or S and S), they will **repel** or push each other away!
