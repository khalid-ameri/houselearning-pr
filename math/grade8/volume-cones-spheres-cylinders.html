What is Volume? 🌊

  **Volume** is the amount of three-dimensional space an object occupies. We measure volume in cubic units (e.g., cubic inches, cubic centimeters).

  The formulas for three common shapes are:

       
  • **Cylinder:** $$V = \pi r^2 h$$
    •    
  • **Cone:** $$V = \frac{1}{3} \pi r^2 h$$
    •    
  • **Sphere:** $$V = \frac{4}{3} \pi r^3$$

  Where **V** is volume, **r** is the radius, and **h** is the height. Notice the relationship: the volume of a cone is exactly one-third the volume of a cylinder with the same radius and height!
