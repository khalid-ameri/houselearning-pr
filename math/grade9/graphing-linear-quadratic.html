What Are Linear & Quadratic Equations? 🚀

  **Linear equations** create a straight line when graphed. They are in the form $$y = mx + b$$, where **m** is the slope and **b** is the y-intercept.

  **Quadratic equations** create a parabola, a U-shaped curve. They are in the form $$y = ax^2 + bx + c$$. The value of **a** determines if the parabola opens up or down.
