What are One-Step Equations?

An equation is a mathematical statement that shows two expressions are equal. A **one-step equation** requires just one operation to solve for the unknown variable.

  The key is to use **inverse operations** to "undo" what's been done to the variable.   The goal is to get the variable all by itself on one side of the equals sign.   $$x + 5 = 12$$   The variable, x, has 5 added to it. To "undo" this, we subtract 5 from both sides.   $$x + 5 - 5 = 12 - 5$$   $$x = 7$$
