What are Inequalities?

An inequality is a mathematical statement that shows a relationship between two expressions that are not equal. Unlike equations, which have one specific solution, inequalities have a whole **set of solutions** that satisfy the condition.

  The four main inequality symbols are:  

       
  • $$<$$ : **less than**
    •    
  • $$>$$ : **greater than**
    •    
  • $$\le$$ : **less than or equal to**
    •    
  • $$\ge$$ : **greater than or equal to**
    •
