Intro & Concept

To divide fractions, we use the simple rule: **Keep, Change, Flip**. We **keep** the first fraction, **change** the division sign to multiplication, and **flip** the second fraction to its reciprocal. Then, we solve it like a multiplication problem! ๐ฅณ
